Est. 2012

Randslines Nepal Pvt. Ltd.

Randslines Nepal Pvt. Ltd. is a leading international freight forwarding company committed to delivering comprehensive logistics solutions with a customer-focused and hands-on approach. With a mission to ensure the trouble-free global movement of goods, we have developed a strong network of affiliates and business alliances spanning across major international trade hubs.

Recognized for consistent, reliable, and quality service, Randslines Nepal has earned the trust of numerous global corporations and retailers. Our company’s reputation is built on our ability to adapt to clients’ evolving needs and our dedication to timely, transparent communication—an essential element in the fast-paced world of international trade.

We provide a full suite of services, including air freight, ocean freight, project shipment, inland haulage, customs brokerage, and logistics consulting.
